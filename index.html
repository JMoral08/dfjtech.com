Web development refers to the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications that are accessible via the internet or an intranet. It involves various tasks such as designing, coding, programming, and deploying websites, along with ensuring their performance, functionality, and user experience.

Web Application Development

Content Management System (CMS) Development

Building websites using content management systems that allow clients to easily manage and update their content (without needing technical expertise).

Web Design Services

Creating aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly designs for websites.

Web Hosting and Domain Services

Hosting and domain registration services to ensure the website is available online.

Services

Web development services refer to the professional services offered by agencies or freelance developers to design, build, and maintain websites and web applications. These services can vary greatly depending on the specific needs of a business, organization, or individual. Web development services can cover everything from simple static websites to complex e-commerce platforms or custom web applications.

Web Analytics and Reporting

Providing insights into website performance, user behavior, and other key metrics

Custom Website Development

Tailored web development solutions designed to meet specific needs. This often includes designing and developing the front-end and back-end from scratch.

E-commerce Website Development

Development of online stores or e-commerce platforms where users can browse products, make purchases, and complete transactions.

Web Security Services

Protecting websites and web applications from cyber threats.

Website Maintenance and Support

Ongoing services to keep a website running smoothly and securely after launch.

Mobile App Development

Some web development agencies also offer mobile application development services, either as a companion to their web applications or as standalone mobile-first applications.
